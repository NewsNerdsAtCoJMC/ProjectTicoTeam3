OUR MISSION

To provide the most accurate traffic, parking, and construction data to the community.

ABOUT

Lincoln Outdoors was founded at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln, by a group of students in the Journalism College. This idea came to them from countless hours in the downtown area of Lincoln, during heavily populated times. They wanted to make it easy for the Lincoln Community to have a simple way to look up when the downtown area was busy during an event, how to avoid it, where to park, and where construction is during that time.
